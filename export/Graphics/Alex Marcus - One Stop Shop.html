One Stop Shop

Once certain ideas land in my head, they live there rent-free until I finish Photoshopping.  I call these "One Stop Shops" because, as a rule, I only stop once: when the image is done.  This process generates quick, fun, and unapologetically nonsensical expressions of a moment in time.  Out of context they are non-sequitors, but in the moment they always get a laugh.
